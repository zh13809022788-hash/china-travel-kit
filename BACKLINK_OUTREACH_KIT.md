## Quora / Reddit answer pattern

1. Answer the question directly in 2-4 sentences.
2. Give a short checklist or step-by-step answer.
3. Add one link only when it genuinely expands the answer.
4. Disclose affiliation when appropriate.

Example:

For most tourists, the safest payment setup is Alipay or WeChat Pay linked to a foreign Visa or Mastercard, plus a small cash backup. Set it up before flying, because identity checks and bank verification are easier on stable internet. I wrote a practical guide and checker here if useful: https://chinatripbox.com/tools/payment-checker/

## Monthly routine

- Week 1: Find 30 prospects and qualify 10.
- Week 2: Send 10 personalized emails.
- Week 3: Answer 5 forum/Quora/Reddit questions with helpful, non-spammy replies.
- Week 4: Publish one new linkable article or tool, then follow up with previous prospects.

Track every contact in a spreadsheet: site, URL, contact, date, status, target page, anchor, result.
