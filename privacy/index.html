Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 10, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how ChinaTripBox ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and safeguards information when you visit this website. By using this site, you agree to the practices described below.

Information We Collect

We do not require you to create an account or submit personal information to read our content. We may collect limited, non-identifying information automatically, such as browser type, device, pages visited, and approximate location derived from your IP address, for the purpose of understanding site usage and improving our guides.

If you contact us by email, we may receive your name, email address, message content, and any information you choose to include. We use this information only to respond to your request, review corrections, or manage partnership enquiries.

If you use a future AI-assisted travel help feature, your question and related context may be processed by a third-party AI provider so that an answer can be generated or summarized. Do not submit passport photos, bank card numbers, passwords, PINs, one-time verification codes, or other sensitive personal information.

Cookies

This site uses cookies and similar technologies to enable core functionality, remember your preferences, measure traffic, serve advertising, and protect site performance. You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings, though some features may not work as intended if you do.

For visitors in regions where consent is required, including the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, and Switzerland, we may use Google's certified consent management tools or another Google-certified consent management platform to request and store your cookie and advertising choices.

Advertising

We display advertisements to keep this site free. Third-party vendors, including Google, may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visits. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

Google and its partners may process data for ad measurement, fraud prevention, frequency capping, personalized advertising, and non-personalized advertising, depending on your location and consent choices. You can learn more about how Google uses information from sites and apps that use its services at Google's partner sites policy.

Analytics and Performance

We may use privacy-conscious analytics, hosting logs, or performance tools to understand traffic trends, detect errors, and keep the site fast and reliable. These tools may collect technical information such as page views, device type, browser, referring page, and general region.

Affiliate Disclosure

Some links on this site are affiliate links. If you click one and make a purchase, we may earn a commission at no additional cost to you. We only recommend products and services we believe are genuinely useful to travelers.

Third-Party Links

Our guides link to external websites and services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these third-party sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

AI-Assisted Features

We may use AI tools to answer simple travel questions, summarize requests, or route complex issues for human review. AI responses may be incomplete or inaccurate and should be checked against official sources when the decision is important.

Your Choices

You can manage cookies through your browser settings, use Google Ads Settings to control ad personalization, and contact us if you want to ask about information you previously sent to us by email.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information handled through this site. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children's Privacy

This site is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please reach us via our contact page.
