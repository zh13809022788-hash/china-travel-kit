China Travel Content Series

Explore China through five themed series: food, history, modern life, nature, and culture — each designed to inspire and inform foreign travelers.

Food & Drink

Food of China

From street stalls to banquet halls — a foreigner's culinary journey through eight great cuisines, regional breakfasts, hot pot styles, and the dishes worth crossing a city for.

Explore series History

History of China

Ancient sites decoded for travelers — the Great Wall, Terracotta Army, Forbidden City, Silk Road, and the stories behind what you're actually looking at.

Explore series Modern Life

Modern China

What surprises foreigners most — cashless streets, the world's fastest trains, megacity scale, super apps, and the everyday tech that defines life in China today.

Explore series Nature

Nature of China

Avatar mountains, fairy-tale lakes, karst landscapes, and the Tibetan plateau — China's most spectacular natural sites with practical guides for foreign visitors.

Explore series Culture

Culture of China

Tea ceremony, Spring Festival, face and guanxi, calligraphy, kung fu, and the zodiac — cultural traditions explained for travelers who want to go beyond surface tourism.
