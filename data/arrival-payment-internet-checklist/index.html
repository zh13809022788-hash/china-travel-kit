China Arrival Payment and Internet Checklist

A build-time JSON example showing how ChinaTripBox can publish static pages directly from data_pipeline records.

July 13, 2026 By ChinaTripBox Editorial Team #arrival#payment#internet#data-pipeline

This page is generated from data_pipeline/pages/arrival-payment-internet-checklist.json at Astro build time.

Use the same structure for generated guides, city facts, tool landing pages, and other static data products.

Before departure

Install Alipay, WeChat, your eSIM app, your airline app, and an offline translation app before you fly.

Keep a small RMB cash backup plan because wallet verification, bank fraud checks, and roaming problems can all happen during the first day.

  • Verify your payment wallet before leaving home.
  • Save your hotel address in English and Chinese.
  • Download offline maps or screenshots for the airport-to-hotel route.

After landing

Turn on your data option first, then test payment with a low-stakes purchase before relying on QR payment for taxis, metro tickets, or restaurants.

If your wallet fails, use a staffed counter, hotel front desk, or ATM instead of repeatedly trying the same blocked card.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this JSON page rendered dynamically on the server?

No. Astro reads the JSON during the build and writes a normal static HTML page into dist.

Can data_pipeline generate many pages this way?

Yes. Add more JSON files under data_pipeline/pages and Astro will include them in getStaticPaths during the next build.

Found something outdated or incorrect? Please send a correction with the page URL so we can review it.
