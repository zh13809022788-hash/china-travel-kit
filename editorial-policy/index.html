Editorial Policy

Last updated: July 10, 2026

ChinaTripBox publishes independent travel guides for foreign visitors to China. Our goal is to make practical topics easier to understand, including payments, mobile internet, transport, city planning, and everyday travel logistics.

How We Research

We use official sources where available, provider documentation, app help centers, public policy pages, traveler reports, and hands-on checks when possible. For topics that change frequently, such as payment limits, eSIM availability, visa-free entry rules, and app support, we aim to verify details before publishing or updating a guide.

How We Write

Our articles are written for real travelers, not for search engines alone. We prioritize clear steps, common mistakes, realistic caveats, and plain-English explanations over generic travel inspiration.

Updates and Corrections

China travel information can change quickly. We review important guides periodically and update pages when we find outdated steps, changed prices, new app behavior, or revised rules. If you spot an issue, please contact us with the page URL and what you believe needs correction.

Advertising and Affiliate Links

ChinaTripBox may earn money from advertising and affiliate links. Commercial relationships do not determine our core editorial advice. When a page includes affiliate links, using those links may earn us a commission at no extra cost to you.

Independence

ChinaTripBox is not affiliated with the Chinese government, Google, Apple, Alipay, WeChat, China Railway, telecom carriers, airlines, banks, or any visa office unless clearly stated. Brand names are used for identification and practical traveler guidance.

Limitations

Our content is general travel information. It is not legal, immigration, financial, medical, tax, or emergency advice. Always confirm official requirements with the relevant government agency, airline, payment provider, bank, telecom provider, or service operator.

Contact

To suggest a correction or ask about our editorial process, please use our contact page.
