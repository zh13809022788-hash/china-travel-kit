Before choosing a city

Check the logistics stack

  • Payments: set up Alipay, WeChat Pay, and cash backup before smaller-city travel.
  • Internet: choose eSIM, roaming, or local SIM before relying on maps and translation.
  • Transport: compare trains, airport transfers, DiDi, and metro QR codes.
  • Timing: check weather, holidays, and crowd pressure by month.
