  • Transport: airport routes, metro, DiDi, taxis, and high-speed rail basics

Safety rules

Keep sensitive data out

  • Do not send passport photos, full card numbers, passwords, PINs, or one-time codes.
  • Do not ask anyone to log in to your accounts or complete identity checks for you.
  • Do not use this for legal, immigration, medical, banking, or emergency advice.
  • Check important rules with official sources, airlines, banks, telecoms, or app operators.
